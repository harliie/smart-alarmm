SAFETY COMES FIRST About Our Application

The Smart Alarm app is your very own mobile bodyguard. A multiple mode of personal protection app for iPhone and Android. When the Smart Alarm is activated on your mobile, it automatically sends a personal GPS panic alarm to our qualified monitoring staff in times of danger and directly calls HOTLINE numbers in the area you are in. The signal pinpoints your GPS location, enabling us to send emergency services directly to your aid. Smart Alert application employs the latest innovation in mobile personal emergency response technology. Readily accessible via Apple and Android App Stores. Once registered with our Smart Alarm App, our monitoring station provides a personal panic alarm for those on the move. Our state of the art monitoring station allows custom tailoring of response instructions, to cater for customers with medical conditions, Crime cases or a history of personal security issues. Activating the Smart Alarm button initiates a call through to our 24 hour monitoring centre, where our staff will stay on the line to ascertain the problem, and dispatch emergency services as required.

Our very own staff, managers, and everyone in the team would always be there for any kind of trouble you get into when our app is activated and is alarmed. Our team will make sure that we send an alert straight to the monitoring center for immediate action when app is active. Of course our main goal would be giving our customers or users a peace of mind and personal protection wherever you may be.
