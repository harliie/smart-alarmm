If people needs an urgent attention regarding fire,crime, health problems and others, if they don't know emergency hotlines there would be a lot of problem.Then the producers came up with the idea what if we create an application that will detect emergency hotlines so that people can access them wherever they are.

Project OVERVIEW

Smart Alert application employs the latest innovation in mobile personal emergency response technology. Readily accessible via Apple and Android App Stores. Once registered with our Smart Alarm App, our monitoring station provides a personal panic alarm for those on the move. Our state of the art monitoring station allows custom tailoring of response instructions, to cater for customers with medical conditions, Crime cases or a history of personal security issues. Activating the Smart Alarm button initiates a call through to our 24 hour monitoring centre, where our staff will stay on the line to ascertain the problem, and dispatch emergency services as required.
